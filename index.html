Go

I started playing Go back in highschool. After introducing it to a few of my friends, we even bought some scrap lumber and made our own boards. It's a game I love playing, and I love teaching it to other people. Despite this, I'm also not very good at it.

While researching CoffeeScript, I decided this would be a perfect test bed. Go is a simple game with few rules, but it still has its intricacies that make it a great way to learn the language. This was also my first time working with the HTML5 canvas, but after finishing, I am now much more comfortable with both technologies.
